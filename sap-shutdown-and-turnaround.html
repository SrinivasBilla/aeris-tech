About Massetic Shutdown and Turnaround Solution

Turnaround Management is an extremely complex and critical process for the process Industries. And it is also a cumbersome process when managed with disparate tools.

Massetic Shutdown and Turnaround Management solution is a revolutionary tool for SAP customers as it brings all STO processes within SAP. This provides agility in decision making, transparency throughout the processes highlighting bottlenecks and issues.

Massetic STO customers had been observing more than 10% reduction in cost and more than 5% reduction execution time.

Product Features

SCOPING & BUDGETING

STO planners can prepare worklists from scratch, ready templates, or based on history. Ad-hoc tasks can be added easily. Open notifications and SAP preventive maintenance work orders from SAP EAM can be added. A flexible approval process with real-time calculated budgets can help finalize the scope of your plant turnaround.

PLANNING & SCHEDULING

Complete visibility of resources from SAP and the ability to create PO and PRs with a few clicks during planning. Activities can be quickly scheduled on the grid or with a powerful Gantt Chart. STOP4 helps in resource leveling. Additional information, attachments, or tasks can be easily added, and roles can be assigned quickly. SAP work order scheduling can be done to determine the project's critical path.

EXECUTION

The turnaround solution supports role-based access to current and upcoming tasks. Technicians and maintenance in-charge can update the status of tasks efficiently, which helps in complete transparency on the status, material availability, critical path, costs, etc. Mobile Add-on solution for confirmation and automated/customizable alerts help in controlling the deviations.

CLOSURE & ANALYSIS

Multiple reports for analysis during planning and execution and post-STO solution completion. Planning completion and readiness for the start of the STO projects. Multiple dashboards for management. Customers can enhance or create new reports using their preferred tools. Completeness check before the closure of the plant turnaround.
