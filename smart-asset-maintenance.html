Smart Enterprise Asset Management Solution

Plan. Dispatch. Report

Increased uptime with efficient preventive and breakdown maintenance

About the Product

SEAM4 is an SAP-based asset maintenance solution, a seamless planning board that supports the maintenance team in managing work orders and assignment of technicians with intelligent assistance and a powerful unique Gantt Chart. Smart asset maintenance helps increase efficiency in the maintenance of assets, timely work completion, and higher asset performance, as well as improvement in the quality of repair jobs.

The solution ensures maximum uptime of assets at an optimized cost, which is the key goal of the plant maintenance teams. In addition, modern and intelligent tools can assist the maintenance team in realizing the goals effectively by bringing information in simple ways for consumption and actions.

Product Features

PLANNING

Complete visibility of all SAP information on a planning board helps prioritize tasks and resources. SEAM4 eliminates the need for multiple applications or screens as it provides all information on a single planning board. Work orders can be modified to maintain spares / PRT, plan resources & maintain related documents. In addition, planners can define release strategies and create post-processing actions.

SCHEDULING

Planners can decide when to execute each of the work orders. They can check for prerequisites such as planned leaves of technicians, availability of spare parts, and Shared Resources. Easy visualization of current load levels of technicians and understanding of overlaps for PRTs help optimize scheduling. Flexible zoom levels help in the micro-planning of the work orders.

DISPATCHINGT

Smart enterprise asset management solution SEAM4 enables flexible management of resources across work centers and supports in right technicians' assignment to jobs. Planners can minimize overbooking and overtime scenarios with all the information available in a single place and configurable alerts.

EXECUTION / MONITOR

Single screen execution support. It helps in checking the real-time status of work orders. Maintenance in charge can assign or reassign technicians based on availability, reserve material for work, equipment lockout, manage deviations, and leaves of technicians. The planning board has several visual features to ease understanding of the work orders' status.

INTEGRATED MATERIAL MANAGEMENT

Integrated material management is a powerful feature that helps check availability and seamlessly creates purchase requests for materials and other resources with only a few clicks from the planning process. Integrated material management supports the timely availability of resources.
