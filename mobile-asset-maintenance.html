Mobile Asset Management Solution

Inspect. Execute. Notify.

Increase wrench time and asset uptime with a robust mobile App

About the Product

MOAST4 is an enterprise-class, modern mobile solution for SAP EAM users. It comes with a modern and easy-to-use interface. MOAST4 takes care of both inspections and work order management. It comes with powerful offline capabilities, in-built device monitoring features, Barcode/RFID support, camera integration, etc. The solution can be easily modified for specific processes of organizations. It integrates seamlessly with SAP and other third-party solutions like document management or collaboration systems.

Product Features

INSPECTION MANAGEMENT

Technicians can get routes, schedules, and equipment locations on their devices. Other details like equipment history and measurement points with limits can be accessed from the mobile application. Manual and auto notification creations, equipment calibration, and time confirmation are some other key features to ease inspection management & make it completely paperless.

WORK ORDER MANAGEMENT

Work Order list (both corrective and SAP Predictive maintenance) with equipment locations, equipment history, and details of permits and approvals can be easily accessed from the mobile device. Technicians can get design documents, manuals, parts, and PRTs required on mobile devices. Technicians can also create new notifications and confirm time and resource consumption from the mobile application. Technicians can record the consumption of spare parts with batch or serial numbers.

SPARE PARTS MANAGEMENT

Technicians can manage local inventories of spare parts & tools and issue / return spare parts. Real-time transparency of usage and availability of PRTs and tools allows optimization of resources and tools available to complete the work orders on time. Options to manage parts in serialized or batch and record consumption of spare parts allow transparency across all departments.
